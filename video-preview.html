Godzilla: King of monsters

About

"Godzilla: King of the Monsters" is an action-packed monster film that follows the story of the ancient super-species Godzilla as he clashes with a host of other gigantic creatures, including Mothra, Rodan, and the three-headed King Ghidorah. Directed by Michael Dougherty, the film is a sequel to the 2014 "Godzilla" movie and is part of the MonsterVerse franchise. The plot revolves around the agency Monarch as they face off against these colossal creatures, who are threatening to wreak havoc on humanity and the world at large. Caught in the middle of the chaos are the members of the Russell family, including Dr. Mark Russell, a scientist studying the creatures, and his ex-wife Dr. Emma Russell, who has developed a device called the "Orca" that can communicate with the Titans.
