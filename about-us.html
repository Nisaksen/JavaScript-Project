About us

Welcome to Square Eyes, where we're on a mission to bring the world of cinema closer to you. More than just a marketplace for movies, we're a vibrant community of film aficionados who share a deep love for storytelling. Our platform is a haven for those who seek out the hidden gems, the indie masterpieces, and the bold visions of small filmmakers.
At Square Eyes, we believe in the power of cinema to inspire, provoke, and unite us. That's why we're committed to curating a diverse selection of films that celebrate creativity, diversity, and the art of storytelling. Whether you're a seasoned cinephile or just starting your cinematic journey, you'll find something to ignite your passion for film here.
Join us as we explore the rich tapestry of cinema, support emerging talent, and connect film lovers from every corner of the globe. Together, let's celebrate the magic of movies and the joy of discovering something new.
